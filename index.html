Frequently Asked Questions

Is my contribution to The Tyee tax deductible?

At this time, contributions to The Tyee are not tax-deductible. The Tyee is a non-profit organization and we use all of our revenue raised to go towards more journalism. We are working towards achieving qualified donee status that would allow us to issue tax receipts for donations. We will update our Tyee Builders when we have received that status.

Are you a Qualified Canadian Journalism Organization (QCJO)? Can I claim my Tyee Builders payment as a qualifying subscription on my taxes?

We are a QCJO but Tyee Builders is not a qualifying subscription expense and is not eligible for a tax credit because subscriptions are meant to provide access past a paywall. Tyee Builders is not a subscription, it is a supporters program.

How can I make changes or stop my monthly contribution?

You can change the amount you're giving us monthly or stop subscribing at any time. Just log in to your member account at account.thetyee.ca any time to make changes. Or if you're having trouble, you can contact us by email, phone (604-689-7489) or toll-free (1-844-301-6677).
